#ONA16Connect

This app was created by Charlie Smart and Aaron Albright as part of the 2016 ONA Student Newsroom. Conference attendees are based on a Twitter list created by the Online News Association. Data was scraped from Twitter using Python. The app is largely JavaScript based, with a Flask back end and a healthy dose of D3.js.
